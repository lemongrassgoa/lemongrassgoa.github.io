We have Joy Dawson on guitar and lead male vocals, Malaika de Souza as our female vocalist, and Anand Lobo on viola, mandolin, and voice. Both Anand and Joy fill in on lead or backing vocals when necessary, for some of the best two- and three-part harmony you'll hear on the classic music you love. We have performed at The Edge Bar at Alila Diwa, BITS-Pilani Goa, the Oak Barrel, Fisherman's Wharf, BENO, The Southern Deck, and weddings and corporate events in North and South Goa.

Our music ranges from genuine "old" oldies from 1600s-1700s classical music (Bach, Mozart, Beethoven, Handel, etc), up to '70s and '80s classic rock (The Eagles, The Beatles, Pink Floyd, Eric Clapton, etc.), up to very recent top hits (Coldplay, The Weeknd, Eric Hutchinson, Hozier) - and quite a few jazzy / bluesy / instrumental numbers to spice things up.
